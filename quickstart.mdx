---
title: "Quick start"
description: "Access JyPS and complete your first request in under 5 minutes"
---

This guide walks you through logging in to JyPS and submitting your first exit pass request as an employee. The entire process takes less than 5 minutes.

<Note>
  After **3 consecutive failed login attempts**, your account is locked for **1 minute**. The lockout timer counts down on screen and the form re-enables automatically when it expires. You cannot bypass the timer — wait for it to reach zero.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the login page">
    Navigate to the JyPS application URL provided by your institution and go to `/login`. You will see the **Sistema JyPS** login form with fields for your institutional email and password.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your institutional credentials">
    Use the email address assigned to you by your institution. Credentials follow this format:

    ```text
    usuario@utez.edu.mx
    ```

    Enter your email in the **Correo Electrónico** field and your password in the **Contraseña** field, then click **Ingresar**.

    If you do not have credentials, contact your system administrator — accounts are not self-registerable.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Reach your role's dashboard">
    After a successful login, JyPS redirects you to the panel for your assigned role:

    | Your role | URL you land on |
    |---|---|
    | Administrator | `/administrador` |
    | Area Manager | `/jefe-area` |
    | HR | `/recursos-humanos` |
    | Employee | `/trabajador` |
    | Security Guard | `/guardia` |

    Each panel only shows the features relevant to your role. You cannot navigate to another role's panel.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the exit pass request form">
    From your **Employee dashboard** (`/trabajador`), you will see two action cards:

    - **Pase de Salida** — for exit pass requests
    - **Justificante** — for absence justifications

    Click the **Pase de Salida** card. This takes you to `/trabajador/solicitar-pase`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in the request form">
    The form pre-fills your name, institutional email, and today's date. You need to provide:

    1. **Hora de Salida** — the time you plan to leave. Select it from the time picker.
    2. **Detalles del Pase** — a written description of your reason for leaving.

    <Tip>
      The **Detalles del Pase** field requires a minimum of **25 characters** and a maximum of **255 characters**. The character counter below the field turns red when you are below the minimum. A good entry includes the reason, destination, and your estimated return time — for example: *"Cita médica en clínica del IMSS, regreso aproximadamente a las 15:00."*
    </Tip>

    The form validates the details field on every keystroke. You cannot submit if the minimum length is not met.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit your request">
    Click **Enviar Solicitud**. JyPS registers the request and returns you to your previous screen.

    Your request is now in **pending** status and cannot be used to exit yet — it must be approved first.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for approval and collect your QR code">
    Your area manager receives the request on their dashboard and approves or rejects it.

    - **Approved**: a QR code is generated for your pass. View it from your request history at `/trabajador/historial`. Present the QR code to the security guard at the exit.
    - **Rejected**: the request is closed. You can submit a new one if needed.

    Check your request history at any time using **Historial** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens at the exit

Once your pass is approved, the security guard scans your QR code in the **Guardia** panel (`/guardia`). The system validates the code and confirms the pass is active and authorized. You are then cleared to leave.

<Note>
  Do not share your QR code. Each code is tied to your specific approved request and is only valid for the date and time listed on it.
</Note>
