---
title: "Exit passes (Pases de Salida)"
description: "Request an exit pass and track its approval and QR code"
---

An exit pass (Pase de Salida) allows an employee to leave the institution's premises during working hours. The employee submits a request through the system, an administrator approves or rejects it, and a QR code is generated upon approval for presentation to the security guard at the exit.

## Lifecycle overview

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit the request">
    Navigate to your dashboard and click the **Pase de Salida** card. Fill in the required fields and click **Enviar Solicitud**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pending approval">
    Your request enters the **Pendiente** state. You can see it in your [request history](/features/request-history) under the **Pases de Salida** tab. While the request is pending you can edit or delete it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Administrator reviews the request">
    The area manager or administrator reviews the request and either approves or rejects it. You will receive a notification when a decision is made.
  </Step>
  <Step title="QR code generated">
    If approved, the system generates a unique QR code for your pass. The pass transitions to the **Aprobado** state and a **Ver Código QR** button appears in your history.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Present the QR code at the exit">
    Open your history, locate the approved pass, and tap **Ver Código QR**. Show the code to the security guard at the exit point.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Exit registered">
    The security guard scans the QR code to register your exit. The pass status changes to **Usado**, confirming the exit has been recorded.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If an approved pass is not used before the return time specified in the request, it transitions to a **Caducado** (expired) state and the QR code button is no longer shown.
</Note>

## Filling in the form

Navigate to the form from Dashboard → **Pase de Salida**. The form has the following fields:

| Field | Editable | Required | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Nombre Completo | No | — | Pre-filled from your account; read-only |
| Correo Institucional | No | — | Pre-filled from your account; read-only |
| Fecha de Salida | No | — | Set to today's date automatically; read-only |
| Hora de Salida | Yes | Yes | Time picker; you choose when you plan to leave |
| Detalles del Pase | Yes | Yes | Free-text description, 25–255 characters |

### Detalles del Pase

This field accepts a free-text description of your exit. Use it to explain the reason for your departure, your destination, and your estimated return time. A live character counter is shown to the right of the field.

<Warning>
  The **Detalles del Pase** field requires a minimum of **25 characters**. The system will not submit the form if the description is shorter than 25 characters, and will display:

  ```
  Debe tener al menos 25 caracteres
  ```

  The maximum length is **255 characters**. If you exceed this, the system shows:

  ```
  No puede exceder 255 caracteres
  ```

  The counter turns red when you are below 25 characters, yellow when you are above 200, and gray when within the valid range.
</Warning>

## After submission

Once submitted, your pass appears in your [request history](/features/request-history). A note below the form confirms:

> Your request will be sent for approval. You will receive a notification once it is approved by your administrator.

You can cancel the form at any time using the **Cancelar** button, which returns you to the previous screen without submitting.

---

## QR code details

The QR code is generated by the system after the administrator approves the pass. It is visible only for approved passes that have not yet expired. To view it:

1. Go to your [request history](/features/request-history).
2. Select the **Pases de Salida** tab.
3. Find the approved pass and click **Ver Código QR**.
4. A modal displays the QR code. Present this to the security guard at the exit.

<Tip>
  Keep the history page open on your phone before approaching the exit gate to avoid delays while the page loads.
</Tip>
